# Layer Protocol

**Species:** Layer (Egg-Laying Chicken)
**Production Cycle:** 72-78 weeks (18 months)
**Traditional Remedies:** NO (Commercial focus - pharmaceutical protocols only)

---

## Overview

Layers are raised for egg production with a long production cycle. Strict pharmaceutical protocols ensure egg safety and consistent production. Traditional remedies are NOT recommended due to:
- Egg withdrawal requirements (consumers eat eggs daily)
- Long production cycle requires consistent protocols
- Commercial food safety standards
- Predictable egg quality needed

## Withdrawal Periods for Layers

### EGG WITHDRAWAL IS CRITICAL

| Medication | Egg Withdrawal | Meat Withdrawal | Notes |
|------------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| Amprolium (Coccidiostat) | 0 days | 0-24 hours | Safe for layers |
| Sulfadimethoxine | 10 days | 10 days | Avoid during production |
| Fenbendazole | 0 days | 0 days | Safe for layers |
| Oxytetracycline | 7 days | 7 days | Discard eggs 7 days |
| Tylosin | 5 days | 5 days | Discard eggs 5 days |
| Vitamins/Electrolytes | 0 days | 0 days | Always safe |

**App Behavior for Layers:**
- Tracks egg withdrawal separately from meat withdrawal
- Shows: "Discard eggs until [date]" when withdrawal active
- Calculates eggs to discard based on flock size
- Warns before giving medications with egg withdrawal

## Emergency Protocols (Layers)

### Egg Drop Syndrome
```
SIGNS: Sudden 10-30% drop in production, soft/shell-less eggs

TREATMENT:
- Multivitamins - 1.5 tbsp/gal for 5 days
- Calcium boost in feed
- Check for Newcastle (may need vaccination)

WITHDRAWAL: 0 days for vitamins
```

### Coccidiosis in Layers
```
SIGNS: Bloody droppings, reduced production

TREATMENT:
Day 1-5: Amprolium - 2 tbsp/gal
Day 6-7: Multivitamins

WITHDRAWAL: 0 days (amprolium safe for eggs)
NOTE: Avoid sulfa drugs - 10 day egg withdrawal
```

### Respiratory Infection
```
SIGNS: Sneezing, drop in production

TREATMENT (vet guidance):
Day 1-5: Tylosin - 1 tsp/gal

WITHDRAWAL: 5 days - DISCARD ALL EGGS
Calculate: 5 days × daily production = eggs to discard
```

## Notes

- **NO traditional remedies** - egg safety requires pharmaceutical protocols
- **Egg withdrawal is critical** - consumers eat eggs daily
- **Long production cycle** - consistent protocols essential
- **Calcium is key** - shell quality affects marketability
- **Monthly deworming** - layers are on ground longer, more parasite exposure
