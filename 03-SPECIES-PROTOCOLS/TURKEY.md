# Turkey Protocol

**Species:** Turkey
**Production Cycle:** 12-20 weeks (meat), 72+ weeks (eggs)
**Traditional Remedies:** YES (Culturally appropriate, validated options included)

---

## Overview

Turkeys are raised in both commercial and traditional/mixed farming systems. This protocol includes:
- Standard pharmaceutical protocols
- Validated traditional remedies
- Turkey-specific considerations (Blackhead disease, heat sensitivity, stress)

**Key Differences from Chickens:**
- More susceptible to Blackhead (Histomoniasis)
- Higher stress sensitivity
- Earlier Fowl Pox vaccination (Week 4 vs Week 8)
- Larger birds = more water consumption
- More prone to respiratory issues

## Blackhead Prevention Protocol

**Blackhead (Histomoniasis) is the #1 killer of turkeys**

```
BLACKHEAD PREVENTION:

Pharmaceutical:
- Metronidazole or similar (where legal)
- Give every 2 weeks throughout production
- Dose: Per veterinary guidance

Environmental:
- NEVER raise turkeys with chickens (chickens carry Heterakis worm)
- Keep turkeys off ground where chickens have been
- Regular deworming (Heterakis worm carries Blackhead)
- Clean, dry bedding

Traditional Support:
- Garlic water (antimicrobial support)
- Oregano (natural antiprotozoal)
- Apple cider vinegar (gut health)

SIGNS OF BLACKHEAD:
- Yellow/sulfur-colored droppings
- Droopy, depressed birds
- Darkened head (hence "Blackhead")
- Rapid death (24-48 hours)

EMERGENCY: If Blackhead suspected, treat IMMEDIATELY
```

## Traditional Remedies for Turkeys

### 1. Garlic (Allium sativum) - HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

**Uses:** Antimicrobial, Blackhead support, respiratory, immune

**Preparation:**
```
GARLIC WATER TREATMENT:

Fresh Garlic:
- Crush 8-10 cloves per liter of water
- Let steep for 4 hours minimum
- Strain and provide as drinking water

Dosage: Full strength for treatment, half for prevention
Duration: 5-7 days treatment, 2-3 days weekly prevention
Frequency: Weekly during high-risk periods

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

**Why Important for Turkeys:** Garlic has antiprotozoal properties that may help prevent Blackhead alongside pharmaceutical treatment.

### 2. Oregano (Origanum vulgare)

**Uses:** Natural antiprotozoal, respiratory support, gut health

**Preparation:**
```
OREGANO WATER TREATMENT:

Fresh Oregano:
- Steep 50g fresh oregano in 1 liter boiling water
- Cool and strain
- Add 100ml per liter drinking water

Dried Oregano:
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano per gallon water
- Steep for 2 hours, strain

Duration: 3-5 days
Frequency: Every 2 weeks (alternate with garlic)

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

**Research:** Oregano oil (carvacrol) shown to have antiprotozoal activity against Histomonas.

### 3. Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV)

**Uses:** Gut health, pH balance, mild antimicrobial

**Preparation:**
```
ACV WATER TREATMENT:

Dosage: 1 tablespoon per gallon of water
Duration: 2-3 days per week
Frequency: Ongoing (not daily - can affect calcium)

CAUTION: Do not use with medications (affects absorption)
Do not use in metal containers (corrosion)

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

### 4. Aloe Vera

**Uses:** Stress relief, digestive support, immune boost

**Preparation:**
```
ALOE VERA TREATMENT:

Same as duck protocol:
- Extract gel from 2-3 leaves
- Blend with 1 liter water
- Add 50ml per liter drinking water

Duration: 3-5 days
Use: Arrival stress, post-vaccination, heat stress

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

### 5. Neem (Azadirachta indica)

**Uses:** Natural dewormer, immune support

**Preparation:**
```
NEEM TREATMENT:

Same as duck protocol:
- Boil 100g leaves in 2 liters water
- Cool, strain
- Add 100ml per liter drinking water

Duration: 3 days
Frequency: Monthly (supplement pharmaceutical deworming)

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

## Stress Management (Critical for Turkeys)

Turkeys are MORE stress-sensitive than chickens:

```
STRESS TRIGGERS:
- Transportation
- Vaccination
- Weather changes
- Loud noises
- Handling
- New birds introduced
- Feed changes

STRESS RESPONSE:
- Always give anti-stress before/after stressful events
- Traditional option: Aloe vera water
- Pharmaceutical option: Vitamin C + Electrolytes
- Duration: 2-3 days before, 2-3 days after

SIGNS OF STRESS:
- Huddling
- Reduced feed intake
- Piling (dangerous - can suffocate)
- Feather pecking
- Increased mortality
```
