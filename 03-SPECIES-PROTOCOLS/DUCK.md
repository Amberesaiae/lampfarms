# Duck Protocol

**Species:** Duck (Meat and Egg)
**Production Cycle:** 8-10 weeks (meat), 72+ weeks (eggs)
**Traditional Remedies:** YES (Culturally appropriate, validated options included)

---

## Overview

Ducks are raised in both commercial and traditional/mixed farming systems. This protocol includes:
- Standard pharmaceutical protocols for commercial operations
- Validated traditional remedies for mixed/smallholder farms
- Species-specific considerations (higher water needs, different diseases)

---

## Water Consumption Table

**Ducks drink 1.5-2x more than chickens** due to:
- Need to submerge head for eye/nostril cleaning
- Higher metabolism
- Swimming behavior (if water access provided)

| Week | Per Bird (ml) | 50 Birds | 100 Birds | Notes |
|------|---------------|----------|-----------|-------|
| 1-2 | 150 | 7.5L (2 gal) | 15L (4 gal) | 3x chick rate |
| 3-4 | 250 | 12.5L (3.3 gal) | 25L (6.6 gal) | Rapid growth |
| 5-6 | 350 | 17.5L (4.6 gal) | 35L (9.2 gal) | Grower |
| 7-8 | 400 | 20L (5.3 gal) | 40L (10.6 gal) | Finisher |
| 9+ | 450 | 22.5L (6 gal) | 45L (12 gal) | Adult |

**Layer Ducks (Week 20+):** 500ml per bird per day

## Duck-Specific Disease Profile

Ducks have DIFFERENT diseases than chickens:

| Disease | Risk Level | Prevention |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Duck Viral Hepatitis | HIGH (Week 1) | Vaccination critical |
| Duck Plague | HIGH (endemic areas) | Vaccination |
| Coccidiosis | LOWER than chickens | Less aggressive protocol |
| Newcastle | MODERATE | Optional vaccination |
| Aspergillosis | MODERATE | Good ventilation |

## Traditional Remedies for Ducks

**These remedies are validated by research and culturally appropriate for duck production:**

### 1. Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis)

**Uses:** Immune support, digestive health, mild infections

**Preparation:**
```
ALOE VERA WATER TREATMENT:

Fresh Aloe:
- Cut 2-3 mature leaves
- Extract gel (avoid yellow latex)
- Blend with 1 liter water
- Strain and add to drinking water

Dosage: 50ml aloe solution per liter of drinking water
Duration: 3-5 days
Frequency: Monthly for prevention, or during stress

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

**When to Use:**
- Arrival stress (alternative to commercial anti-stress)
- Mild digestive upset
- General immune support
- Heat stress (cooling effect)

### 2. Neem (Azadirachta indica)

**Uses:** Natural dewormer, immune booster, mild infections

**Preparation:**
```
NEEM LEAF WATER TREATMENT:

Fresh Leaves:
- Collect 100g fresh neem leaves
- Boil in 2 liters water for 15 minutes
- Cool and strain
- Add 100ml concentrate per liter drinking water

Dosage: 100ml neem tea per liter water
Duration: 3 days
Frequency: Every 2-4 weeks for parasite prevention

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

**When to Use:**
- Natural deworming (supplement to pharmaceutical)
- Mild bacterial infections
- Immune support during rainy season
- Skin/feather health

### 3. Papaya Seeds (Carica papaya)

**Uses:** Natural dewormer (proven effective against roundworms)

**Preparation:**
```
PAPAYA SEED DEWORMER:

Dried Seeds:
- Collect seeds from ripe papaya
- Dry in sun for 2-3 days
- Grind to powder
- Mix 1 teaspoon per 10 birds in feed

Fresh Seeds:
- Blend fresh seeds with water
- Add to drinking water (1 papaya worth per 20 birds)

Duration: 3 days
Frequency: Monthly (can alternate with pharmaceutical dewormer)

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

**Research Validation:** Studies show papaya seeds effective against Ascaridia galli (roundworm) in poultry.

### 4. Garlic (Allium sativum)

**Uses:** Antimicrobial, immune booster, respiratory support

**Preparation:**
```
GARLIC WATER TREATMENT:

Fresh Garlic:
- Crush 5-6 cloves per liter of water
- Let steep for 2-4 hours
- Strain and provide as drinking water

Garlic Powder:
- 1/2 teaspoon per gallon of water

Duration: 3-5 days
Frequency: Weekly during disease outbreaks, monthly for prevention

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

**When to Use:**
- Respiratory issues (mild)
- General immune support
- During disease outbreaks in area
- Rainy season prevention

### 5. Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

**Uses:** Anti-inflammatory, liver support, immune booster

**Preparation:**
```
TURMERIC WATER TREATMENT:

Fresh Turmeric:
- Grate 50g fresh turmeric
- Boil in 1 liter water for 10 minutes
- Cool, strain, add to drinking water

Turmeric Powder:
- 1/4 teaspoon per gallon of water

Duration: 5-7 days
Frequency: During stress, illness recovery

NO WITHDRAWAL REQUIRED
```

## When to Use Traditional vs Pharmaceutical

| Situation | Recommendation | Reason |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| Arrival stress | Traditional (Aloe) OR Pharmaceutical | Both effective |
| Mild digestive upset | Traditional (Aloe, Garlic) | Gentle, no withdrawal |
| Severe diarrhea | Pharmaceutical | Faster action needed |
| Routine deworming | Alternate Traditional/Pharmaceutical | Reduces resistance |
| Coccidiosis outbreak | Pharmaceutical | Traditional too slow |
| Respiratory (mild) | Traditional (Garlic) first | Try natural first |
| Respiratory (severe) | Pharmaceutical | Antibiotics needed |
| Immune support | Traditional (any) | Safe, effective |
| Pre-sale period | Traditional ONLY | No withdrawal concerns |
