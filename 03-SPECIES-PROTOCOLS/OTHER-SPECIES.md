# Other Species Protocols

**Species Covered:** Quail, Guinea Fowl, Geese
**Traditional Remedies:** YES (All species - appropriate for smallholder/mixed farming)

---

## QUAIL PROTOCOL

### Overview

- **Production Cycle:** 6-8 weeks (meat), 8+ weeks (eggs)
- **Characteristics:** Small size, high coccidiosis susceptibility, simple vaccination
- **Traditional Remedies:** YES

### Water Consumption

| Week | Per Bird (ml) | 100 Birds | 500 Birds |
|------|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| 1-2 | 20 | 2L (0.5 gal) | 10L (2.6 gal) |
| 3-4 | 30 | 3L (0.8 gal) | 15L (4 gal) |
| 5+ | 40 | 4L (1 gal) | 20L (5.3 gal) |

**Note:** Small volumes - measure carefully!

### Medication Schedule (100 Quail, 8 Weeks)

| Week | Day | Medication | Water | Dose |
|------|-----|------------|-------|------|
| 1 | 1-3 | Anti-stress + Glucose | 2L | 0.5 tbsp |
| 1 | 4-6 | Coccidiostat | 2L | 0.5 tbsp |
| 1 | 7 | Newcastle (Live) | 1L | 0.25 tbsp |
| 2 | 8-10 | Multivitamins | 3L | 0.75 tbsp |
| 2 | 11-13 | Coccidiostat | 3L | 0.75 tbsp |
| 2 | 14 | HB1 or IB | 1.5L | 0.5 tbsp |
| 3 | 15-17 | Coccidiostat | 3L | 0.75 tbsp |
| 3 | 21 | Newcastle booster | 1.5L | 0.5 tbsp |
| 4 | 28 | Deworming | 3L | Per label |
| 6 | 42 | Deworming | 4L | Per label |

**Layer Quail (Week 6+):**
- Newcastle booster every 3 months
- Deworm monthly
- Coccidiostat as needed

### Quail-Specific Notes

- **Very susceptible to coccidiosis** - aggressive prevention needed
- **Small water volumes** - use measuring spoons, not tablespoons
- **Fowl Pox rarely needed** - usually skipped
- **Simple vaccination** - Newcastle focus
- **Short cycle** - minimal medication overall

### Traditional Remedies for Quail

**Garlic Water (scaled down):**
```
2 cloves crushed per liter water
Duration: 3 days
Use: Immune support, mild infections
```

**Aloe Vera (scaled down):**
```
25ml aloe solution per liter water
Duration: 3 days
Use: Stress, digestive support
```

## GUINEA FOWL PROTOCOL

### Overview

- **Production Cycle:** 12-16 weeks (meat), 72+ weeks (eggs)
- **Characteristics:** Hardy, stress-sensitive, respiratory prone, foraging behavior
- **Traditional Remedies:** YES

### Water Consumption

| Week | Per Bird (ml) | 50 Birds | 100 Birds |
|------|---------------|----------|-----------|
| 1-2 | 75 | 3.75L (1 gal) | 7.5L (2 gal) |
| 3-6 | 125 | 6.25L (1.7 gal) | 12.5L (3.3 gal) |
| 7+ | 200 | 10L (2.6 gal) | 20L (5.3 gal) |

### Medication Schedule (50 Guinea Fowl, 16 Weeks)

| Week | Day | Medication | Water | Dose |
|------|-----|------------|-------|------|
| 1 | 1-3 | Anti-stress | 3.75L | 1 tbsp |
| 1 | 4-6 | Coccidiostat | 3.75L | 1 tbsp |
| 1 | 7 | Gumboro | 1.9L | 0.5 tbsp |
| 2 | 8-10 | Multivitamins | 6.25L | 1.5 tbsp |
| 2 | 14 | HB1 | 3.1L | 0.75 tbsp |
| 3 | 21 | Gumboro Plus | 3.1L | 0.75 tbsp |
| 4 | 28 | Lasota | 3.1L | 0.75 tbsp |
| 5 | 35 | Gumboro Plus | 5L | 1.25 tbsp |
| 7 | 49 | Deworming | 10L | Per label |
| 8 | 56 | 1st Fowl Pox | Wing web | - |
| 10 | 70 | Lasota | 10L | 2.5 tbsp |
| 12 | 84 | 2nd Fowl Pox | Wing web | - |
| 13 | 91 | Deworming | 10L | Per label |
| 16 | 112 | 3rd Newcastle | 10L | 2.5 tbsp |

### Guinea Fowl-Specific Notes

- **Similar schedule to layers** - same vaccination pattern
- **Very stress-sensitive** - handle gently, avoid loud noises
- **Prone to respiratory issues** - Newcastle vaccination critical
- **Foraging behavior** - deworm regularly (more parasite exposure)
- **Hardy overall** - fewer disease issues than chickens

### Traditional Remedies for Guinea Fowl

**All remedies from Duck/Turkey protocols apply:**

**Garlic (Respiratory Support):**
```
HIGHLY RECOMMENDED for guinea fowl
5-6 cloves per liter water
Duration: 5 days
Use: Respiratory prevention, immune support
Frequency: Weekly during stress periods
```

**Neem (Deworming Support):**
```
100g leaves boiled in 2L water
Add 100ml per liter drinking water
Duration: 3 days
Use: Supplement pharmaceutical deworming
Frequency: Monthly
```

**Aloe Vera (Stress):**
```
50ml aloe solution per liter water
Duration: 3-5 days
Use: Arrival, handling, weather changes
```

## GEESE PROTOCOL

### Overview

- **Production Cycle:** 10-14 weeks (meat), 72+ weeks (eggs)
- **Characteristics:** Very hardy, minimal vaccination, high water consumption
- **Traditional Remedies:** YES

### Water Consumption

**Geese drink 2-3x more than chickens**

| Week | Per Bird (ml) | 30 Birds | 50 Birds |
|------|---------------|----------|----------|
| 1-2 | 200 | 6L (1.6 gal) | 10L (2.6 gal) |
| 3-6 | 400 | 12L (3.2 gal) | 20L (5.3 gal) |
| 7+ | 600 | 18L (4.8 gal) | 30L (8 gal) |

### Medication Schedule (30 Geese, 14 Weeks)

| Week | Day | Medication | Water | Dose |
|------|-----|------------|-------|------|
| 1 | 1-3 | Anti-stress | 6L | 1.5 tbsp |
| 1 | 7 | Goose Parvovirus | Injection | Per label |
| 2-3 | 8-21 | Multivitamins (weekly) | 12L | 3 tbsp |
| 3 | 21 | Newcastle (optional) | 6L | 1.5 tbsp |
| 4 | 28 | Deworming | 12L | Per label |
| 8 | 56 | Deworming | 18L | Per label |
| 12 | 84 | Deworming | 18L | Per label |

### Geese-Specific Notes

- **Very hardy** - minimal vaccination needed
- **Goose Parvovirus essential** - critical in first week
- **Focus on deworming** - main health concern
- **Natural disease resistance** - fewer medications overall
- **Newcastle only if mixing with chickens**
- **High water consumption** - ensure adequate supply

### Traditional Remedies for Geese

**Geese respond well to traditional approaches due to natural hardiness:**

**Garlic (General Health):**
```
6-8 cloves per liter water
Duration: 3-5 days
Use: General immune support
Frequency: Monthly
```

**Apple Cider Vinegar:**
```
1 tbsp per gallon water
Duration: 2-3 days per week
Use: Gut health, mild antimicrobial
```

**Papaya Seeds (Deworming):**
```
Seeds from 1 papaya per 20 birds
Mix in feed or blend with water
Duration: 3 days
Use: Natural deworming (alternate with pharmaceutical)
Frequency: Monthly
```

**Neem:**
```
Standard preparation
Duration: 3 days
Use: Parasite prevention, immune support
Frequency: Every 2-4 weeks
```
